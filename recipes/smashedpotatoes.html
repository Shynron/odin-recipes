Smashed Potatoes

Description:

Pretty basic smashed potatoes without many ingredients. Great for someone begining their journy cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of potatoes
  • your choice of seasonings

Steps:

  1. Steam the potatoes until fork tender. [Leave the skins on]
  2. Move the cooked potatoes to a bowl and let them slightly cool.
  3. After potatoes are safe to handle, move to a baking dish lined with parchment paper.
  4. Use the bottom of a glass cup and slightly press down on each cooked potato.
  5. Sprinkle on the seasoning of your choice.
  6. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-18 minutes until edges are crispy.
  7. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
